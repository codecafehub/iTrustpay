Frequently Asked Questions

What is iTrustpay?

iTrustpay is a money transfer platform that allows users to send and receive money using just a WhatsApp number, making financial transactions easy, fast, and secure.

How does iTrustpay work?

Simply link your WhatsApp number to your iTrustpay account, and you can instantly send or receive money by entering the recipient's WhatsApp number—no need for bank account numbers or long forms.

Is it safe to use iTrustpay for money transfers?

Yes, iTrustpay uses the latest encryption technologies to ensure that your transactions are secure. We prioritize the safety and privacy of our users' financial information.

Do I need a bank account to use iTrustpay?

While you don’t need to share bank account details with the recipient, you do need to link your bank account to your iTrustpay account for sending or receiving funds

Can I transfer money internationally with iTrustpay?

Currently, iTrustpay supports domestic transfers within [country/region]. We are working on expanding our services to enable international transfers

Are there any fees for using iTrustpay?

iTrustpay charges a small transaction fee depending on the amount being transferred. Full details of fees can be found in the pricing section of our website.

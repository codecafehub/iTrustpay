how it works

Registration

Both the end user and the fulfiller (agent) must be registered with Social Lender.

Choose Your Transaction Channel

Initiate a payment transaction using either SMS or WhatsApp. The iTrust Pay platform translates your transaction request instantly.

Processing the Transaction payment

For deposits, your account will not be immediately impacted.

For withdrawals, your account will be updated in real-time.

Cash Handling by Fulfiller

The fulfiller (agent) manages the cash-related transactions, ensuring smooth operations.

End-of-Day Reconciliation

All transactions with fulfillers are reconciled daily to ensure accuracy and trust.
